26-27July

2025 Winter Warmer

Approx 250km and 4,000m elevation.

On Saturday, the ride meets at Roma Street Parklands goes up to Maleny staying overnight overlooking the beautiful vista of the Glass House Mountains at Tranquil Park Resort.

After a hot breakfast on Sunday morning the ride home takes you along some meandering country roads before returning to Roma Street Parklands.

A limited number of rooms have been reserved so make sure you get in early.

Membership with AusCycling or Bicycle Queensland is required for all riders participating on this ride. An Adult Lifestyle Membership of AusCycling is available from ${{ auscycling.amount|floatformat:'0g' }} per month.

Make some new memories with Lifecycle Cycling Club and the Wombles over a legendary weekend of riding and good cheer.
