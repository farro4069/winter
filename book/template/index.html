6-7July

Winter Warmer

Approx 250km and 4,000m elevation.

On Saturday, the ride goes up to Maleny staying overnight overlooking the beautiful vista of the Glass House Mountains at Tranquil Park Resort.

After a buffet breakfast on Sunday morning the ride home takes you along some meandering country roads before approaching the city.

A limited number of rooms have been reserved so make sure you get in early.

Membership with AusCycling or Bicycle Queensland is required for all riders participating on this ride. An Adult Lifestyle Membership of AusCycling is available from $13 per month.

Make some new memories with Lifecycle Cycling Club and the Wombles for a legendary weekend of riding and good cheer.
