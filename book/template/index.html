6-7July

Winter Warmer

On Saturday, we'll ride up to Maleny and stay overnight overlooking the beautiful vista of the Glass House Mountains at Tranquil Park Resort.

After a buffet breakfast on Sunday morning we'll ride home along some meandering country roads before approaching the city.

A limited number of beds have been reserved so make sure you get in early.

Join Lifecycle Cycling Club and the Wombles for a legendary weekend of riding and good cheer.

$225

    What's included

  • Baggage carriage
  • Riding support
  • Sag wagon
  • Morning tea at Mt Mee
  • Lunch stops
  • Shared platters on arrival
  • Twin share accommodation
  • Buffet breakfast

Accommodation
