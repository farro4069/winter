Important information

Insurance

It's important that you acknowledge that you ride at your own risk. You should be aware of what is covered by the insurance included in your AusCycling or Bicycle Queensland membership. You may wish to take out additional insurance. You are advised to seek your own independent advice.

    Personal costs not included

  • Snacks, gels, power bars.
  • Lunch on Saturday at Woodford.
  • Refreshments on Saturday evening.
  • Dinner Saturday night.
  • Barista coffees Sunday morning.
  • Morning tea at Wamurran.

Don't want to ride

No problem. If you would like to book accommodation, you will need to register and choose the "Non-rider" option from the Status panel.

Don't need accommodation

Simple. Please register and choose "no accommodation" from the drop down. To cover the incidental costs of providing support vehicles, volunteers and morning tea, you'll be required to pay just $40 for the riding components only - No accommodation and no Sunday morning breakfast.

What to pack

Pack a small backpack that you can carry to the assembly point and return.

You will need toiletries, a change of riding kit, and clothes for Saturday evening. It may be cool so a light-weight (polly fleece or similar) jumper.

A charger for your phone, lights and cycle computer.

Don't forget to carry at least two spare tubes with you on the bike.

Dinner Saturday

Unfortunately, the restaurant has been booked out for a wedding on Saturday night. A reservation has been made nearby at King Ludwig's German Restaurant and Bavarian Beer Bar (less than a kilometre away) with no obligation. You are free to explore other options in Maleny and elsewhere.

How to pay

After you have registered you will receive an invoice by email. Details on payment options will be outlined with the invoice. This must be paid in full by 21 June.

Cancellation policy

The ride will go ahead in light rain. It the forecast is dreadful a week out the organisers will attempt to cancel accommodation. Whatever costs are returned will be distributed to particpants equitably. There is a chance that you will not receive a full refund.

Contact

Please direct any further questions or contact to winter.maleny@gmail.com
